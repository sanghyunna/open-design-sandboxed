# HTML Anything · Product introduction

## The problem we solve

- Markdown is easy to write but poor to share.
- Screenshot posts look rough.
- Publishing platforms all impose different formatting rules.

## Our approach

**Any input → world-class HTML**, powered by your local AI agent.

## Three essentials

1. Reuse the local agent sessions you are already signed into (Claude, Cursor, or Codex); no API key needed.
2. Nine world-class templates for slides, resumes, posters, social cards, and data reports.
3. Copy once to publishing platforms or export PNG.

## Metrics

- A 31 KB deliverable in 80 seconds.
- Nine templates and eight local-agent integrations.
- Zero API keys; reuse local sessions.

## Why now

The best teams are moving from Markdown to HTML. HTML is the final form readers see, and AI makes polished writing practical.
