# testing-safety-alert

White background, red and amber warning colors, striped hazard edges, bold red strike-through text, and pills. Inspired by the `.focus` black-on-white block, hero quote box, and high-contrast treatment in `20260412-AI-testing-and-safety/xhs-ai-testing-safety-v2.html`, but pushed toward warning, risk-control, and incident-report language.

**Visual traits:** 45° red-and-black diagonal warning stripe at the top, a secondary bottom stripe, oversized red struck-through type, L1/L2/L3 color cards (green/amber/red), alert boxes with circular indicators, a dark policy-YAML code block with a red left border and `bad` keyword highlights, red/green checklist boxes, and a Q1 incident bar chart.

**Use when:** discussing safety, risk control, incident reviews, red-team testing, pre-launch AI evaluation, or policy as code; you need the audience to immediately feel that this is serious.
