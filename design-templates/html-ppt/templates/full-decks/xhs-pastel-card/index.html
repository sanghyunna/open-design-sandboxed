Four little escapes
03 · 08

Four small things
to hand fully to AI

01

Reply to simple acknowledgement emails

They do not need your attention. Let AI answer in your voice and save 40 minutes a week.

02

Book restaurants, change flights, find routes

Outsource it in one sentence. You choose the final option instead of scrolling through ten apps.

03

Turn meeting recordings into action items

Recording → summary → to-do list in one step. You only need to confirm and sign off.

04

Organize last week's 300 photos

Group by event, choose ten highlights, and write captions. Archiving finally becomes automated.

A prompt to automate
acknowledgement emails

# auto-reply skill
when email matches "received / okay / confirmed / thanks":
  reply:
    tone: "warm, brief, not too corporate"
    max_lines: 2
    sign_with: "— Lewis"

always_skip:
  - from: ["family", "partner", "close friends"]
  - contains: ["urgent", "contract", "payment"]

# Saves 38 minutes a week, tested
