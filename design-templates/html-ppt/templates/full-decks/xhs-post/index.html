✨ aha moment

💡 The truth

It is not too little sleep;
it is waking at the wrong time.

Your body works in roughly 90-minute cycles.
If an alarm pulls you out of deep sleep,
even nine hours can feel like none.

The key: wake at the end of a cycle.

5 / 9
1

Count backward from wake-up

👉 Formula

Wake-up time − 90min × N − 15min to fall asleep
= your bedtime tonight

Example: wake at 7:00

→ bed at 23:15 (4 cycles)
→ bed at 00:45 (3 cycles)

Ten minutes of morning light

☀️ Open the curtains / take a short walk

Daylight stops melatonin and helps you wake up for real. It works on cloudy days too.

⏰ stronger than coffee
