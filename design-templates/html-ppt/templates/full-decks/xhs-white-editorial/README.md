# xhs-white-editorial

White editorial style, emphasized focus blocks, and macaron soft-card groups. Inspired by the rainbow top strip and oversized title in `20260409-upgraded-knowledge-base/social-carousel/v2-white/slide_01_cover.html`, plus the `.focus` black-on-white emphasis and macaron card system in `20260412-AI-testing-and-safety/xhs-ai-testing-safety-v2.html`.

**Visual traits:** Pure white background, a ten-color rainbow top strip, oversized 80–110px titles with light negative tracking, gradient brand text (purple → blue → green → orange → pink), macaron soft-color cards (`soft-purple` / pink / blue / green / orange), a capsule tag with dot, black `.focus` callouts, and a lightly shadowed hero quote box.

**Use when:** you need a white-background content post that can work as a social carousel or landscape deck, with dense copy and high-priority keywords that need to be visible immediately.

**Source inspiration:** the 20260409 social-carousel v2 white cover + the 20260412 AI testing-and-safety v2.
